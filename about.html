How did we start P R O C O D E ?

It all began in 2004 when a group of four school-friends decided to teach children in their locality about computers. Yes! At a time when more than half of the Indian population didn't know anything about the bulky-old-sloth.

Promoted! Blackboard to Smartclass.

The year of 2010 was a milestone in our journey. Not only did our looks change, but also we grew to a family of nearly 500.

And Miles to Go, Before We Sleep . . .

The rising demand of e-learning in the post-Covid economy was like a catalyst to our growth. We are commited to meeting the demand as well as the aspirations of our students and be a significant factor in their success.
